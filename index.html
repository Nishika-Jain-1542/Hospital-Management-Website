About Sanjeevni Health & Care Hospital

Sanjeevni Health & Care Hospital was established in 2015 with the goal of providing reliable, affordable, and high-quality healthcare services to the community. Since its beginning, the hospital has focused on creating a safe and comfortable environment where patients feel cared for and respected.

The mission of Sanjeevni Health & Care Hospital is to improve the health and well-being of people through ethical medical practices and patient-centered care, while its vision is to become a trusted healthcare institution known for quality, innovation, and social responsibility. The hospital offers a wide range of healthcare services including general medicine, emergency care, diagnostic services, outpatient consultation, and basic surgical support.

About the Founder

Sanjeevni Health & Care Hospital was founded under the guidance and leadership of Br. Deepa Didi from Vidisha, Madhya Pradesh, and Br. Nidhi Didi from Guna, Madhya Pradesh. Both founders have played a key role in establishing the hospital with a strong belief in serving society through ethical and compassionate healthcare. Their dedication and clear vision have been the foundation on which Sanjeevni Hospital has grown as a trusted healthcare institution focused on patient well-being and dignity.


Their vision is to provide quality healthcare services that are accessible, honest, and patient-centered. Under their leadership, Sanjeevni Hospital promotes a culture of respect, safety, and continuous improvement among doctors, nurses, and staff members. Their guidance inspires the entire medical team to work with commitment and empathy, ensuring that every patient receives care with professionalism, trust, and humanity.
