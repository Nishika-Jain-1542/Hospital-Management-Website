About Sanjeevi Health & Care Hospital

Sanjeevi Health & Care Hospital is committed to delivering high-quality medical services with a patient-first approach. The hospital focuses on ethical healthcare practices, modern treatment methods, and experienced medical professionals to ensure the well-being of society.

About the Founder

The hospital was founded under the leadership of Br. Deepa Didi and Br. Nidhi Didi, with a vision of compassionate healthcare.
The founder of Sanjeevi Health & Care Hospital envisioned a healthcare system that is accessible, reliable, and affordable. With a strong belief in service to humanity, the founder laid the foundation of this hospital to bridge the gap between quality healthcare and the common people.
